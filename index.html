About Us

At Adhere360, we are committed to tackling Nigeria's pressing healthcare challenges by improving medication adherence. Non-adherence rates are estimated to exceed 50% across chronic conditions like diabetes and hypertension in Nigeria, leading to significant health risks and higher healthcare costs1. Our platform is designed to make healthcare management seamless, accessible, and effective for everyone by leveraging cutting-edge technology and partnerships with local pharmacies and healthcare providers.

Our Services

Discover how Adhere360 is transforming healthcare in Nigeria with innovative, technology-driven solutions for medication adherence and patient support.

AI-Powered Reminders

Customized notifications powered by AI to ensure timely medication intake, helping patients adhere to treatment plans with precision.

Real-Time Adherence Tracking

Smart trackers and IoT-enabled devices log medication intake, enabling real-time monitoring for healthcare providers.

24/7 Patient Support

Around-the-clock access to health coaches and educational resources, offering continuous support and guidance.

Predictive Analytics for Health

Data-driven insights predict adherence risks, enabling early interventions to improve patient outcomes.

Incentive-Based Adherence

Rewards for consistent adherence, providing patients with redeemable perks to motivate health-positive habits.

Offline Access & Multi-Language Support

Ensuring accessibility through offline functionality and support in multiple languages, reaching underserved communities.
